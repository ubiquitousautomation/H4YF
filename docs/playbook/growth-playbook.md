# Growth Playbook — HEAT4YAFEAT

## Growth Philosophy

Whatnot growth is compound. Every follower you earn today is a notification recipient for your next 100 shows. Prioritize follower acquisition aggressively in months 1–3, even at the cost of short-term GMV. The channel that wins long-term is the one that built the room, not the one that wrung margin out of a small audience.

---

## Organic Growth Levers (No Ad Spend)

### 1. The Follow Ask (Every Show, 3 Times)

The single highest-ROI action on Whatnot. Say this out loud, in these words, at these moments:

- **Show open:** "If you're new tonight, hit that follow button — it's free and that's how you know every time we go live with fresh heat."
- **Mid-show break:** "Quick reminder for anyone who just joined — we're HEAT4YAFEAT. Follow us so you never miss a drop."
- **Show close:** "We're back live [day] at [time]. If you haven't followed yet, now's the time."

Track follows per show. The goal is 10+ net new followers per show in month 1.

### 2. Cross-Promote Your Existing Platforms

You have buyers on eBay and StockX who already trust you. Convert them:

**eBay Actions:**
- Add to every listing description: "Want to bid live? Follow us on Whatnot: [link]"
- Update your eBay store banner with Whatnot live date/time
- Add Whatnot to your eBay About page
- Message recent high-value buyers: "Hey — we're going live on Whatnot [date] with [heat]. Would love to see you there." (Keep it personal, not spammy)

**StockX:** Limited opportunity for direct outreach, but your StockX buyer history validates your curation taste. Screenshot high-value completed sales and use them as social proof content.

### 3. TikTok + Instagram Reels (The Flywheel)

You don't need a massive following. You need clips that make people want to tune in live.

**Content that works:**
- "Watch this [shoe] go from $1 to $[final price] in 60 seconds" — clip the auction close from your Whatnot replay
- "Authentication breakdown on this [shoe]" — 30-second process video, builds trust
- "Show day setup" — behind the scenes, humanizes the brand
- "This is why I passed on [shoe] from [source]" — opinion content, builds expertise positioning
- "Found this for $[X], it's worth $[Y]" — the sourcing/flip story is evergreen

**Posting cadence:**
- 1 clip per show day (clip from the show)
- 1 educational or behind-the-scenes piece mid-week

Always end with: "We go live on Whatnot every [Thursday + Saturday] — link in bio."

### 4. Whatnot Algorithm Plays

The platform's internal discovery surfaces sellers based on activity signals. Maximize them:

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Show frequency | Never cancel a scheduled show — reschedule instead |
| Completion rate | Always end shows — even low-viewership shows |
| Rating | Respond to all messages within 4 hours, ship same day |
| Saves/wishlists | Ask buyers to save your profile between shows |
| Clip shares | Clip highlight moments post-show, re-share in Whatnot Stories |

### 5. Whatnot Community Participation

Your show isn't your only presence on the platform. Engage between shows:

- Watch other sneaker shows occasionally — leave thoughtful comments (not promotions)
- Engage with the Whatnot sneaker community Discord or groups if available
- Be known in the community before you need something from it

## Paid Growth (Month 2+)

### Whatnot-Promoted Shows

Whatnot offers promoted placement in the feed. Use it selectively:

- **When to promote:** Special event shows, $1 start blitz shows, drop-day shows tied to a new release
- **When not to promote:** Regular weekly shows — let organic build first
- **Budget:** Start at $50/show promoted spend. Evaluate CPF (cost per follower) — target < $1.00

### Instagram/TikTok Ads

- Run 7-day video ad campaigns leading into a major show
- Targeting: 18–35, interests = sneakers, Jordan Brand, Nike, hypebeast, streetwear, Whatnot
- Creative: Auction close clip or authentication video with "Going live [date]" overlay
- Budget: $5–$10/day. Scale what converts followers, kill what doesn't.

## Collab Show Strategy

Collab shows are the fastest non-paid audience growth mechanism on Whatnot.

### How It Works
Two sellers go live together. Both audiences see both sellers. Net result: ~20–40% of the partner's audience follows you.

### Who to Target for Collabs

| Partner Type | Example | Why |
|-------------|---------|-----|
| Complementary streetwear seller | Hats, vintage, jerseys | Same buyer, no inventory overlap |
| Accessories seller | Shoe care, laces, bags | Adjacent to your category |
| Mid-size sneaker seller (similar GMV) | Someone at your level, not above | Fair value exchange |
| Regional seller | Different geography, same product | Non-competing audiences |

**Who NOT to collab with:** Your direct competitors. Don't grow their audience.

### Collab Outreach Template

DM on Whatnot or IG:

> "Hey [name] — big fan of what you're doing. I run HEAT4YAFEAT, we specialize in authenticated Jordans and streetwear. I think our audiences would love a collab show — interested in exploring? Happy to host first."

Offering to host first lowers the barrier. They get on your stream, you get their followers.

## Community Building

### Repeat Buyer Cultivation

Your 40% repeat buyer rate target (month 3) requires active cultivation, not just good product.

Tactics:
- **Thank you cards in every package:** Handwritten or printed. "Thanks for watching live — we'll have heat next [day]. Follow us for the drop."
- **Shout regulars out on stream:** If someone buys 3+ items in one show, call them out by name. They'll be back.
- **Post-show buyer message:** Send a generic (non-spammy) thank-you via Whatnot message for first-time buyers.
- **VIP preview list:** Build an IG close friends or group chat for top buyers. Give them 24-hour preview of next show lot.

### The First 100 Followers

These are your founding community. Treat them like insiders:
- Remember their names on stream
- Give them first pick on presales
- Ask them what they want to see — literally ask on stream "What should I bring next week?"

People don't just buy shoes. They buy belonging to a community of people who know shoes. That's your product.

## 90-Day Follower Growth Roadmap

| Week | Action | Follower Target |
|------|--------|----------------|
| 1 | Account setup + IG announcement | 50 |
| 2 | First show + clip posted to TikTok/IG | 100 |
| 3 | Second show + eBay cross-promotion active | 150 |
| 4 | Third show + first buyer outreach messages | 200 |
| 6 | First collab show | 400 |
| 8 | $1 blitz show (promoted) | 600 |
| 10 | Second collab show | 900 |
| 12 | Third collab + consistent 3x/week schedule | 1,500 |

These are targets, not guarantees. The floor for month 1 is 100 followers. If you're below 100 at 4 shows in, the show format or promotion needs to change before you scale frequency.
